About Travia Travels

Connecting people with unforgettable adventures across the globe.

Company Background

Travia Travels was founded in 2015 by three adventurous friends who shared a bold vision: to transform how people experience the world. What began as a local tour company in San Diego quickly evolved into a global brand, built on the belief that travel should go beyond landmarks—it should forge lasting connections, shift perspectives, and inspire personal growth.

In our early days, we focused on handcrafted itineraries for small groups, combining adventure with authentic cultural encounters. Word of mouth spread fast, and soon we were expanding to Southeast Asia, Europe, and South America—always guided by our mission to connect people through purposeful, immersive travel.

At the heart of Travia is a commitment to sustainability and community. We partner with local guides, support small businesses, and promote responsible tourism practices that preserve the environments and cultures we visit. Whether you're hiking through the Andes, exploring temples in Thailand, or joining a family dinner in Tuscany, every journey with Travia is designed to enrich both traveler and host.

Today, Travia is recognized as a pioneer in boutique adventure travel, with a growing family of passionate explorers, repeat travelers, and change-makers. We're proud of how far we've come—and even more excited for where we're headed next.
