Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers! Check out our most asked queries below.

What documents do I need to travel?
You’ll need a valid passport, visa (if required), and any health certifications for your destination.
Do you offer customized tours?
Absolutely! You can personalize every aspect of your travel—from destinations to daily schedules.
What payment methods are accepted?
Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, bank transfers, and even cryptocurrency on select trips.
How far in advance should I book?
Booking 4–6 weeks in advance is recommended. We also handle last-minute travel!
Is my personal information safe?
Yes, we use secure SSL encryption and are GDPR-compliant.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel up to 14 days before departure for a full refund. Terms vary by package.
Are group discounts available?
Yes! Great deals for groups of 5+ people. Contact us for a custom quote.
Do your guides speak multiple languages?
Our guides speak English, Spanish, French, Mandarin, and more depending on region.
What COVID-19 safety measures do you follow?
We follow the latest health protocols including sanitization, contactless check-ins, and distancing when needed.
Can I choose my accommodation?
Yes, from budget hotels to 5-star resorts. We'll tailor the options to your comfort level.
Are your tours family-friendly?
We offer many family-friendly destinations, with kid-safe activities and meals.
What if I need help during my trip?
We provide 24/7 support—call, text, or chat with us anytime during your journey.

Still have questions?

Reach out via our Contact Page — our travel team is here to help!
